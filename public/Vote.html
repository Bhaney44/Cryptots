About Us

We created this project for the MIT 2021 Bitcoin Expo Hackathon as part of the Cryptots team. Our goal was to create a DOA(Decenrtalized Autonnomous Organization) that also used the Skynet Servers to record transactions and other data. We decided to make a financial-based DOA because of the inherent tendendcy of financial organizations to be conducted in a traditional top down-manner. Our competition areas were:

The Adoptiblity track
The Algorand Challenge
The Skynet Challenge
