Algorand Autonomous: Decentralizing financial investments

Algorand Autonomous is a decentralized autonomous organization that manages to retain the security of a fund while giving members the independence of a investment broker.

View our white-paper!

Smart Contracts that abide by cryptographic consensus protocols

Through use of the Algorand system, we made a smart contract that allows users to make investments through a secure manner to our multiple portfolios. We also plan on integrating validation so that validators can be rewarded for any work they choose to do.

Voting Algorithms that allow everyone to have a say

Unlike other funds that operate as DAOS, Cryptots is truly decentralized, making for a equal voting system where your say is proporitional to your stake in the fund

Time your investment accoridngly with the Algorand cryptocurrency

All of our members and validators conduct transactions throught the use of the algorand cryptocurrency. To aid them in this process, we developed a neural network that manages to track the value of the algorand cryptocurrency, so that our members and validators can choose when they want to participate.

Decentralized: Now and Forever

The Algorand Autonomous fund will always be decentralized. Anyone can invest, at any time, by simply interacting with the smart contract.

Our voting stake algorithm ensures that you get an equal say based on your stake, and you can record your own transactions to the decentralized Skynet server in order to have a proof of work.
